About Us

Discover our story and passion for authentic Indian cuisine

Our Story

Founded in 1985, Anand Restaurant has been serving authentic Indian cuisine for over three decades. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most beloved restaurants in the city.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to bring the true flavors of India to our community. Every recipe we use has been passed down through generations, ensuring that each dish carries the authentic taste and tradition of Indian cooking.

Today, we continue to honor our heritage while embracing modern culinary techniques, creating a perfect blend of tradition and innovation that keeps our customers coming back for more.

Meet Our Head Chef

Chef Rajesh Anand

With over 25 years of culinary experience, Chef Rajesh Anand is the heart and soul of our kitchen. Born and raised in Punjab, he learned the art of cooking from his grandmother and has been perfecting traditional Indian recipes ever since.

Chef Rajesh believes that cooking is not just about following recipes, but about putting love and passion into every dish. His expertise in regional Indian cuisines and innovative approach to traditional cooking has earned him recognition from food critics and customers alike.
